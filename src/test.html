Day Exercise Sets Reps Tempo Rest (seconds) Notes
Day 1 Squat 3 6-8 2-1-2-1 90-120 Focus on proper form. Depth is key. If back squatting is painful, try front squats or goblet squats.
Bench Press 3 6-8 2-1-2-1 90-120 Control the descent. Use a spotter if needed.
Bent-Over Row 3 8-10 2-1-2-1 60-90 Maintain a flat back. Pull the bar to your lower chest. Can substitute with chest supported rows to minimize lower back stress.
Overhead Press 2 8-12 2-1-2-1 60-90 Standing or seated. Control the movement.
Dumbbell Romanian Deadlifts 2 10-12 2-1-2-1 60-90 Focus on hamstring and glute activation. Keep your back straight. Light bend in the knee.
Calf Raises 3 15-20 2-1-2-1 45-60 Choose a variation (standing, seated, single leg).
Day 2 Deadlift 1-2 3-5 2-1-2-1 120-180 IMPORTANT: Warm up thoroughly. Focus on perfect form. Do NOT exceed 2 working sets if you are fatiguing too early. Use this sparingly.
Incline Dumbbell Press 3 8-10 2-1-2-1 60-90 Focus on upper chest activation.
Pull-Ups (or Lat Pulldowns) 3 AMRAP (or 8-12) 2-1-2-1 60-90 If you can't do pull-ups, use lat pulldowns with a weight that allows you to perform 8-12 reps. If doing pull-ups, add weight once you can exceed 12-15 reps per set.
Lunges 3 10-12 (per leg) 2-1-2-1 60-90 Can be walking lunges or stationary lunges. Choose a variation that works best for you.
Dumbbell Shoulder Press 2 10-12 2-1-2-1 60-90 Focus on controlled movements.
Barbell Curls 2 8-12 2-1-2-1 60-90 Use good form; minimize swinging.
